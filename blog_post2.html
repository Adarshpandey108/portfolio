Understanding Cyber Security

By Adarsh Pandey - September 26, 2024

What is Cyber Security?

Cyber Security refers to the practices and technologies designed to protect networks, devices, and data from unauthorized access, attacks, or damage. In an increasingly digital world, where personal and corporate data is constantly at risk, cyber security is a critical field that ensures the safety of information and prevents breaches.

Common Cyber Threats

Cyber threats come in many forms, including malware, phishing attacks, ransomware, and denial-of-service attacks. These threats can target individuals, corporations, or even governments. The key to cyber security is understanding these risks and implementing the right protective measures to mitigate them.

How to Protect Yourself Online

Basic protection steps include using strong, unique passwords, enabling two-factor authentication, keeping software updated, and being cautious of unsolicited emails or links. More advanced techniques involve setting up firewalls, regularly auditing your security systems, and educating yourself about the latest cyber threats.
